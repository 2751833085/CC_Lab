About Time

The theme of the second task is How to determine the time?

I think the best way to determine the time is through the sun (except for very high-dimensional areas). There is an old Chinese saying that "work at sunrise, rest at sunset". Sunny weather is very suitable for work, and dark environment is more suitable for sleeping. Therefore, I directly made a clock with the sun as the standard.
