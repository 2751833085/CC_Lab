Background Story


From the sky descends a mighty hand,
People scatter, no place to stand.
They fight with grit, against the night,
Praying for miracles, seeking the light.

The Blue God rises, their faith to renew,
A spark of hope, a flame so true.
With wonders revived, and courage drawn,
The Blue departs, but faith lives on.

Red and Blue clash in fierce array,
Blood paints the skies, and clouds turn gray.
Power meets power, fury meets flame,
The world holds its breath, as they play their game.


Rule:

As a god, you rise with power bold,
Guiding the Blue, a force to hold.
Against the Red, your will commands,
To shape the fate of distant lands.

Time is fleeting, life drains away,
Each second counts in this fierce fray.
With ease you wield fate’s final call,
A world’s destiny rests on your thrall.

In your hands, creation’s might,
To cast in shadow or bring to light.
So play your moves with purpose true,
For all that’s left depends on you.
